General info

Amy is a 5 year old doggo. She is mixed-breed between: Papillon (Kontinental toy spaniel), Maltese and Yorkshire terrier. She is a social dog who loves medium distance walks and a good family doggo. Partly allergy friendly because of her ties to the Maltese and Yorkshire terrier.

Behind the fluff and fame

Amy is a fluffy ball of joy. Her favourite past-times are: sleeping, barking at strangers and cuddling. She recently put on a bit of weight, this does not concern her though as there is now more to love

Fun facts about Amy

Her favourite food is liverpaste

Her least favourite food is salad

She does not like big doggos as this makes her insecure about her own size

She barks at strangers, not because she is angry but because she wants to say hi

Where can i find a doggo which is also best?

There are many amazing doggos out there. There is a lot of responsibility and work to keep a doggo healthy and happy. If you are ready for the resonsability there are many good doggos who need a home. I would recommend using the world wide web and finding local adoption centers and give a doggo a chance for a better life, and becoming a part of your life.
